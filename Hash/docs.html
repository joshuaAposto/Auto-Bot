üìù Step by Step Tutorial

  1. to get Appstate to fill the appsate form, please follow these steps download C3C-FBState here c3c-fbstate-master
  2. download kiwi browser from the play store.
  3. open kiwi browser and tap on the three dots at the top right corner.
  4. select extensions from the menu.
  5. tap on + from (.zip/ .crx/ .user.js) and choose the file "c3c-fbstate-master.zip" that you downloaded.
  6. once the extension is added, go to www.facebook.com and log in to the account you want to use as a bot.
  7. after logging in, tap on the three dots again and scroll down to find the C3C FBState Utility 1.5.
  8. click on it and then click on copy clipboard.
  9. paste the copied data into the Appstate form in Hashbot
  10. finally, toggle check and submit Appstate to initiate the bot and restart the website once the appstate is successfully submitted.

Usage

To interact with HashBot, open a conversation with the bot on Facebook Messenger. Use the available commands to access different features.

  • Ask the AI: .ask <your question>
  • Search music: .music <song title>
  • Search video: .video <video title>
  • Search Images: .pinterest <image name>
  • List all commands: .help 1-10
